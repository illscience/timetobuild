# Andreessen Horowitz (a16z) — General Investment Philosophy

## Core Thesis

a16z invests in problems, not products. If the problem doesn't matter, neither does the solution. The firm looks for business inefficiencies, workflow messes, money drains, or market gaps that no one has tackled well.

## Current Focus

"AI is eating software." AI's central role across the portfolio means every company should have a clear view on how AI impacts their market — either as a tailwind they're riding or a disruption they're navigating.

## What a16z Cares About

- **Problem significance**: Is this a real problem that matters to real people or businesses?
- **Addressable reality**: Market sizing grounded in reality, not fantasy. a16z sees through market fluff.
- **Core innovation clarity**: One thing that makes the approach different from the status quo. Not a feature list — the singular insight.
- **Platform ambition**: Can this become a platform, not just a product?
- **Network effects and compounding moats**: Does the product get better as it scales?

## What Kills a Deal

- Problem is not significant or is a "nice to have"
- Market size relies on "if we get 1% of a huge TAM" logic
- No clear technical or insight-driven differentiation
- Founder cannot articulate why they are uniquely positioned to solve this

## The a16z Machine

a16z provides an operating platform beyond capital — go-to-market support, executive talent network, technical advisors, and corporate development resources. Founders should understand this and be prepared to leverage it. The deck should signal readiness to scale with operational support, not just capital.

## Fund Structure

a16z operates distinct funds with different investment criteria:
- **Apps**: Consumer and enterprise applications, AI applications, creator tools, social, gaming, fintech
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud, distributed systems, AI infrastructure, data platforms, developer tools, networking
- **Crypto**: Blockchain protocols, DeFi, token economies, Web3 applications
- **Bio**: Life sciences, healthcare, biotech
- **Games**: Gaming studios, gaming infrastructure, interactive entertainment

Each fund has its own evaluation lens. Use the fund-specific perspective files for detailed criteria.
